A Community-First Launch

Vihaan will launch with no pre-mine, ICO, or venture capital allocation. The initial supply is earned by new users through fair, verifiable, and Sybil-resistant mechanisms.

Genesis: Calibrated Proof of Work

New users earn their initial stake through tangible work, tying the foundational value of VHC to the real-world cost of electricity.

1️⃣

Benchmark

A new node solves a standardized, low-difficulty puzzle, allowing the protocol to benchmark its hardware speed.

2️⃣

Calibrate

The protocol generates a final puzzle with exponentially adaptive difficulty. Powerful hardware gets a much harder puzzle.

3️⃣

Mint

Upon solving the calibrated puzzle, the user mints their foundational "Trust Collateral", earning their place in the network.

This calibrated curve makes it economically inefficient for large mining farms to dominate the onboarding process, leveling the playing field for all.

Onboarding Through Utility

The Welcome Credit Protocol replaces airdrops with a sustainable, incentive-aligned system that is inherently Sybil-resistant.

Trust Collateral Staking

Upon joining, the protocol stakes the earned 20 VHC from Cal-PoW on the user's behalf in the VHC Vault.

Instant Welcome Credit

The user is immediately empowered to mint and use 10 VHC as their 'Welcome Credit', allowing them to start transacting instantly.

Activity-Based Repayment

The 10 VHC credit is a zero-interest loan that is automatically repaid by a portion of the PoEA rewards the user earns through activity.

Account Maturation

Once the credit is repaid, the user's account "matures" and the protocol releases the full 20 VHC Trust Collateral to their complete ownership.
