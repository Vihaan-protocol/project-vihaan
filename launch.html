An Anchor to the Real World

Vihaan's value is intrinsically anchored to the real-world cost of energy. New currency is created through a perpetual, on-demand process that is open to everyone, at any time.

On-Demand Issuance: Calibrated Proof of Work

Any user can mint new VHC by completing a simple, standardized Proof of Work puzzle. This is not a competitive race; it is a fair and open process for currency creation.

The Price Anchor Effect

This continuous, on-demand issuance mechanism creates a powerful economic equilibrium that naturally resists speculative bubbles and crashes.

When Market Price > Minting Cost

If VHC becomes expensive on exchanges, rational actors will mint their own VHC for the cheaper cost of electricity. This increases the supply, putting downward pressure on the market price, pushing it back toward equilibrium.

üìà

When Market Price < Minting Cost

If VHC becomes cheap on exchanges, no one will expend electricity to mint it at a loss. The creation of new supply halts, and existing market demand puts upward pressure on the price, pushing it back toward equilibrium.
