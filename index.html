Boosting Your Velocity Score

This score measures your economic output. It's not just about volume; smart heuristics reward genuine commerce.

  • Transaction "Freshness": Rewards using coins that have been held for a while, preventing rapid spam-trading.
  • Unique Counterparty Bonus: Earn a multiplier for transacting with new users, actively encouraging network growth.

Securing Your Stability Score

This score, based on the logarithm of your balance, acts as a fairness anchor and spam-prevention shield.

  • Spam Prevention: Rapid back-and-forth transactions result in a near-zero average balance, nullifying the PoEA score.
  • Diminishing Returns: Logarithmic math means wealthy holders can't dominate rewards. Activity is always the primary factor.
