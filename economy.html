Transaction Fees are the Fuel

All rewards paid to the Guardian validators who secure the network are funded entirely by a small fee on every transaction. These fees are collected into a central reward pool each day.

The Anti-Centralization Engine

At the end of each day, the reward pool is distributed through a unique two-step process that makes centralization economically irrational.
